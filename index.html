ABOUT PRISCA MPOYI

Prisca Mpoyi is the wife of Apostle Jonas Mpoyi of Strong and Mighty Jerusalem Church International. They have been married for 11years and they have 3 children in total. At the tender age of 15 she gave her life to Christ and since then has never looked back. She is a worshipper and a seasoned recording artist. She has one studio album to her name and various compilations of gospel music covers.

She has a degree in Bio Science and Chemistry. Since 2018 she has been studying with the International School of Ministry. She is a speaker, a counsellor, a mother, a role model and is very passionate about education for ladies & women. Both her and her husband are from the Republic of Congo and currently reside in South Africa where she serves under her husband in the ministry.
